The US immigration system has a few bugs. This is one of the major ones.

The problem is this: If you lose your passport with a stamped visa abroad you can't come back into USA until you replace the passport and visa!

Hm.. replacing a passport takes a few weeks. Getting a new visa takes a week or two?? Wrong! In order to replace your H1B visa at a US consulate you will need a form I-797 Notice of Action. Most of US keep it right next to a passport so in case of a lost/stolen passport it would go with it.

There is a six month waiting period to replace I-797! Yes. Six months to wait. During the six months you can't come back to the USA unless you get a visitor visa but you can't work that way.
